Toddlers Weekly Swimming Programme

The programme focuses on building water confidence, developing flutter kicks, learning to blow bubbles, and eventually moving towards more advanced skills like kicking and pulling and turning which eventually provides a solid foundation for swimming skills. This approach offers a progressive and structured way to help toddlers gain confidence in the water while introducing them to essential swimming techniques.
