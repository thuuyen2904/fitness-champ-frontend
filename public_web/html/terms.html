Fitness Champs Case Study Terms & Conditions - Swim Programme Terms

1) Definitions

“Centre” refers to Fitness Champs.

“Programme” refers to the swimming lessons and related activities.

“Student” refers to the participant enrolled in the programme.

“Parent/Guardian” refers to the responsible adult of the enrolled student.

2) Enrolment & Slot Confirmation

  • Enrolment is subject to slot availability and confirmation by the centre.
  • Submission of registration does not automatically guarantee placement.
  • Students may only attend classes after payment and confirmation are completed.
  • The centre reserves the right to assign class schedules based on programme capacity.

3) Fees, Term Payment & Proration

3.1 New student payment

All new registrations must be paid in full before the first confirmed lesson unless otherwise stated.

3.2 Renewals

Renewal fees should be settled before the start of the next term to secure the existing slot.

Fees paid are strictly for the confirmed programme term and may not be transferred without approval.

3.3 Late changes

Any approved change may be subject to administrative handling and revised fee treatment where applicable.

4) Programme Durations, Term Dates, Holidays & Attendance

4.1 Programme duration by programme

  • Each programme follows the published lesson structure and duration.
  • Lesson dates may vary based on public holidays, venue access, and centre scheduling.
  • Parents should refer to the latest term calendar communicated by the centre.

4.2 Attendance rules

Students are expected to attend lessons punctually and consistently throughout the term.

5) Missed Classes & No-Show

  • Missed lessons, late arrivals, and no-shows are generally not refundable.
  • Make-up lessons are not guaranteed and remain subject to the centre’s policy and availability.
  • Repeated absences may affect progression and class continuity.

6) Make-Up Options (Medical, Travel & Approved Reasons)

6.1 Eligible reasons

  • Medical reasons supported by appropriate documentation.
  • Travel or exceptional circumstances reviewed case by case.
  • Any other reason expressly approved by the centre.

6.2 Make-up process

  • Requests must be submitted within the timeframe stated by the centre.
  • Approved make-up lessons are subject to schedule and slot availability.
  • Specific coach, venue, or timing cannot always be guaranteed.

6.3 Expiry rules

  • Unused make-up lessons may expire if not utilised within the approved period.
  • Expired make-up lessons are not refundable or exchangeable.

7) Cancellations, Refunds & Credits

7.1 Student-initiated withdrawal

Refunds, if any, are subject to the timing of withdrawal, administrative deductions, and programme policy.

7.2 Centre-initiated changes

Where a class is cancelled or materially changed by the centre, suitable alternatives or credits may be offered.

7.3 Non-refundable items

Administrative fees, reserved slots, and partially consumed programme portions may be non-refundable.

8) Coach Changes, Class Format & Progression

  • The centre reserves the right to assign or replace coaches when necessary.
  • Class format may vary based on level, venue conditions, and operational requirements.
  • Progression decisions are made by the coaching team based on readiness and safety.

9) Health, Medical Fitness & Duty of Disclosure

  • Parents/guardians must disclose relevant medical conditions before participation.
  • Students should only attend if medically fit for swimming activities.
  • The centre may restrict participation if safety concerns arise.

10) Safety Rules, Facility Rules & Behaviour

10.1 Swimming pool and facility management

  • All students must follow pool and venue safety rules at all times.
  • Parents and guardians should ensure children are supervised outside actual lesson time.
  • Disruptive, unsafe, or inappropriate behaviour may result in removal from class.

10.2 Personal belongings

The centre is not responsible for loss or damage to personal belongings brought to the venue.

10.3 Communication

Updates may be communicated through official contact channels including email, messaging, or administrative announcements.

11) Photography, Video & Use of Images

  • The centre may occasionally capture media for internal, progress, or promotional use.
  • Parents should notify the centre if they have specific media-related concerns.

12) Liability, Exclusion & Indemnity (Summary)

  • Participation in swimming activities carries inherent risks.
  • The centre’s liability is limited to the extent permitted by applicable law.
  • Parents/guardians remain responsible for information accuracy and compliance with programme requirements.

13) Personal Data & Communications

By registering, you consent to the collection and use of information necessary for enrolment, communication, class administration, and related support services.

14) Governing Law & Jurisdiction

These terms are governed by the applicable laws and regulations relevant to the operating jurisdiction of the centre.

15) Acceptance

By confirming enrolment, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and accepted these terms and conditions.
